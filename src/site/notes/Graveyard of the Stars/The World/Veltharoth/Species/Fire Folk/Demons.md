Demons are  Unholy Souls or creatures that originate from [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Dimensions/Hell/Hell\|Hell]]. Demons are entities of domination, hunger, and corruption. They reshape environments simply by existing. They serve under [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Moder, Fader & Kin/Glavhor & Kin/Glavhor\|Glavhor]]. They have taken over some of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Great Bog/The Great Bog\|The Great Bog]] and changed it into[[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Great Bog/The Fire Swamps/The Fire Swamps\|The Fire Swamps]].
