Satyrs are a species of half human and half goat people, usually more in tune with magical items. Commonly found in more Humid climates (ex:[[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/The Azure Coast\|The Azure Coast]] & [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Everplains/The Everplains\|The Everplains]]  ) & Groves
