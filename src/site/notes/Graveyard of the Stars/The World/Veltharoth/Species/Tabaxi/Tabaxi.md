Tabaxi are a race of Anthropomorphic Cat people. Tabaxi are driven by curiosity and motion. To remain in one place too long is considered unhealthy. Their cultures emphasize travel, collection of stories, and personal freedom.
They are commonly found in trade routes across [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Everplains/The Everplains\|The Everplains]] and [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/The Azure Coast\|The Azure Coast]], Savannahs like that of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Everplains/Mystic Grasslands/Mystic Grasslands\|Mystic Grasslands]]. and Port cities like [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/Towns & Cities/Lumina's Grace/Lumina's Grace\|Lumina's Grace]].
