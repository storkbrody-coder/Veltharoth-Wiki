 Kitsune are anthropomorphic fox people. Kitsune are ancient shapeshifters bound to illusion, story, and identity. They believe truth is flexible and that names are masks. Age brings power, but also layers of secrecy.
Typically found in the forests of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Everplains/The Everplains\|The Everplains]] and [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/The Azure Coast\|The Azure Coast]].
