Lizard-Folk are Lizard Humanoids, Lizardfolk are pragmatic, emotion-light, and deeply tied to their environment. They view the world through survival, balance, and territory rather than morality. Tradition is instinct, not law.
Typically found in [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Great Bog/The Great Bog\|The Great Bog]], river deltas and mouths, such as those of [[The Sablerun \|The Sablerun ]] and [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Natural Features/Galewyr River\|Galewyr River]].
