Yuan-Ti are a humanoid snake-like race, Yuan-ti are ancient, secretive, and obsessively hierarchical. They believe the world is meant to be ruled by those with the patience to wait and the will to shed their humanity. Many yuan-ti societies are part cult, part empire.
Typically found within secret temple cities in [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Great Bog/The Great Bog\|The Great Bog]] and deep sanctums of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Natural Features/The Underdark\|The Underdark]].
