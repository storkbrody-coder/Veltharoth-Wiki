Dwarves are a humanoid, subterranean species. Dwarves are master builders and historians who believe permanence is the highest virtue. Most dwarves live in Underground cities or Ones with large scale mining (such as [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/Towns & Cities/Obsidian Rest/Obsidian Rest\|Obsidian Rest]]).
