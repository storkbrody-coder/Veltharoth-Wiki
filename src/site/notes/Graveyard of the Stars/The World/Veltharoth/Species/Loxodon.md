Loxodon are large Humanoid Elephants, Typically more magically In-tune. Loxodon are calm, deliberate, and community-oriented. Memory and tradition guide their lives. Loxodon can usually found in more Arid climates such as:[[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Whispering Sands/The Whispering Sands\|The Whispering Sands]] & [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Everplains/Mystic Grasslands/Mystic Grasslands\|Mystic Grasslands]].
