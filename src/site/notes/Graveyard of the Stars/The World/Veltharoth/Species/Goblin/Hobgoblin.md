Hobgoblins are  the larger version of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Species/Goblin/Goblins\|Goblins]], Typically a bit smarter and uglier as well. Hobgoblins are disciplined, militarized, and hierarchical. Where goblins survive, hobgoblins **conquer**. Their societies are built around order, strategy, and obedience to command. Typically found in more fortified settlements of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Deadlands/The Deadlands\|The Deadlands]] and in strategic chokepoints in [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Natural Features/The Underdark\|The Underdark]]
