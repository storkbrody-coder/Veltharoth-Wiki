Goblins are Small humanoid creatures that usually lurk in groups of 3 to a few hundred. Goblins are fast-breeding, clever, and brutally adaptive. They thrive in the cracks of civilization, making use of what others discard. Goblin culture values cunning over strength and survival over honor. Found in [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Deadlands/The Deadlands\|The Deadlands]], the tunnels of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Natural Features/The Underdark\|The Underdark]], and abandoned structures & towns.
