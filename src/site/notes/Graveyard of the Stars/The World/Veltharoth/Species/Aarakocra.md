Aarakocra Are a species of bird people. Aarakocra are aerial nomads who value height, wind, and perspective above all else. They see the ground as temporary and the sky as eternal. Commonly found in the Sky islands of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/Kaelorin's Highlands/Kaelorin's Highlands\|Kaelorin's Highlands]].
