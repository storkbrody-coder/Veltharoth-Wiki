A Race of Turtle People. Tortles are slow-speaking, long-lived wanderers who value experience over possession. they mainly live within the more Arid climates of the world. (ex;[[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Whispering Sands/The Whispering Sands\|The Whispering Sands]].)
