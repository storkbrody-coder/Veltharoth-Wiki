Centaurs are Half-man, Half-horse creatures. Centaurs are nomadic, proud, and deeply territorial. They view land as something to be _traveled_, not owned. they exist within humid climates, and Hilly areas such as [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Everplains/The Everplains\|The Everplains]], [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/The Azure Coast\|The Azure Coast]], & [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/Kaelorin's Highlands/Kaelorin's Highlands\|Kaelorin's Highlands]].
