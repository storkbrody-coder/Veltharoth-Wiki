The rolling crown is a large area of raised land along the easter hills of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Everplains/The Everplains\|The Everplains]]. It dominates the skyline for miles. it has excellent farming. The edge of The Rolling crown is adorned with watchtowers overlooking [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Deadlands/The Deadlands\|The Deadlands]].
