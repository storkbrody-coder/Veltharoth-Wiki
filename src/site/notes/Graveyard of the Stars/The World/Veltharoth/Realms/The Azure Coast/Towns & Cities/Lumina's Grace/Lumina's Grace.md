Lumina's Grace is a major port city along [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/The Azure Coast\|The Azure Coast]], It sits at the mouth of the Galewyr River. 
	Lumina's Grace is named after the goddess [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Assorted Gods & minor deities/Lumina\|Lumina]] after she saved a group of settlers from a horde of  Unrelenting monsters.
The City is divided up into four internal boroughs and 5 external boroughs.
	The Internal boroughs:
		Sunspire
		Thornmere
		Harbourhold
		Gilded Row
	The External boroughs:
		Wolfway Steps
		The Western Slums
		Skyrise Gate
		The Grey Streets
The City is home to the major theocracy of the region: The Church of Radiance, Which worships Nyzawrath. 
Lumina's Grace is also one of the two major trade cities within The Azure Coast, with many popular businesses sprouting from this city.
The City's wall are guarded by "Shield Generators" that are made up of large magical purple crystals. With these a Magical barrier is made around the city preventing any magical or physical damage from being done to it. Thus the only true way to enter the city is through the city gates.
