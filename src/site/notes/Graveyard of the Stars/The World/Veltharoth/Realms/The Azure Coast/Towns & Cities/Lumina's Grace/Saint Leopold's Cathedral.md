Located in [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/Towns & Cities/Lumina's Grace/Lumina's Grace\|Lumina's Grace]].The main church of The Church of Radiance. Home to their leaders: The Twilight Council. Saint Leopold's Cathedral house various pieces of history and religious relics. the building itself is made of a bright white stone, shimmering in the daylight, and seemingly brightening up the night. The building also has five tall spires stretching upwards towards the sky.
