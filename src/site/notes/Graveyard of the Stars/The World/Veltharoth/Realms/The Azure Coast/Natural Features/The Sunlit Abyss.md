The Sunlit Abyss Is a Large Cavern In [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Natural Features/The Underdark\|The Underdark]], That seemingly has no bottom. Floating Islands populate the pit, all of them seemingly having direct sunlight, even without having direct access to sunlight.
