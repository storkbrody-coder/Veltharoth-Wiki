Glimmegrove: This grove on the western area of the region is home to many magical creatures. It is said that the forest "Glimmers & Twinkles at night" however nobody has seen this since those that enter this forest don't usually return.

Fairfax Grove: This grove is home to multiple gnome villages such as Glood. this forest also contains the rare Fairfax Cyprus, which can secret the rare, & sought after Feywild Moot: a magical like sap that, when refined, grants the consumer visions of a lush & wealthy future However when consumed raw Feywild Moot can give the consumer premotions of the apocalypse.

The Emerald Grove: A large Root wall surrounds this grove making it incredibly hard to gain access to. This grove is rumored to have many powerful druids that can draw from Dormun's power themselves.
