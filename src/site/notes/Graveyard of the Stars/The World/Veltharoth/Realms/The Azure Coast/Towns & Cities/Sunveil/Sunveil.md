Sunveil is a major trade hub within [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/The Azure Coast\|The Azure Coast]], with most roads & paths leading through it. 
The City is home to The Church of The Golden Path, Who worships Thalgrin. Although in recent years has accepted The Church of Radiance in for control of the city.
This city, like Lumina's Grace, Has many popular chain taverns & restaurants in the city as well as businesses.
