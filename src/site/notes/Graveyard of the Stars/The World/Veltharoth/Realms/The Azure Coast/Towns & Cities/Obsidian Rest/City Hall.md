The [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/Towns & Cities/Obsidian Rest/Obsidian Rest\|Obsidian Rest]]city hall is where all of the members of the city council convene to decide policies, laws, and projects for Obsidian Rest. The council is made up of 9 Members.
