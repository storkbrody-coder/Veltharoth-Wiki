"Where a kid can beat a kid! " Bull-E-Wugs is a chain sports tavern / restaurant around Veltharoth.  Menus vary from each location, but the entertainment never does:
Child fighting. in the center of each restaurant you can find up to 4 child fighting rings, which you can of course, gamble on.  Most locations are ran and staffed by bullywugs.
