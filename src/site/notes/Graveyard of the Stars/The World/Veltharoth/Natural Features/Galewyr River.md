The Galewyr River is the largest river in Veltharoth. Starting in [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Azure Coast/The Azure Coast\|The Azure Coast]], connecting to [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Natural Features/The Sablerun\|The Sablerun]], then flowing through [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Everplains/The Everplains\|The Everplains]], The [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Everplains/Mystic Grasslands/Mystic Grasslands\|Mystic Grasslands]], and ending in [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Realms/The Whispering Sands/The Whispering Sands\|The Whispering Sands]]. The Galewyr is Crucial to trade Throughout Veltharoth
