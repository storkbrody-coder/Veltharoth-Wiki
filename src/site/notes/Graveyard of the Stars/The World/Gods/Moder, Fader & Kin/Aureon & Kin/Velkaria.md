"The Lady of Death" Velkaria is the daughter of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Moder, Fader & Kin/Aureon & Kin/Nyzawrath\|Nyzawrath]]. she is the goddess of death. Velkaria is rarely spotted riding on a horse made of bone through The Deadlands.
