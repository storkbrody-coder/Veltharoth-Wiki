"The Beast-father"  Kaelorin is the son of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Moder, Fader & Kin/Dormun & Kin/Dormun\|Dormun]]. He is the god of Animals and the Hunt. Typically Kaelorin is depicted as a large Mossy Stagg. One of the Various Major [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Gods\|Gods]].
