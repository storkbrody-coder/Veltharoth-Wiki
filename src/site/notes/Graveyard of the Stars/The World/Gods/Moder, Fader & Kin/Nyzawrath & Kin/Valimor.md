"The Shattered Son" Son of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Moder, Fader & Kin/Nyzawrath & Kin/Aureon\|Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Moder, Fader & Kin/Nyzawrath & Kin/Aureon]]. God of Fallen Glory, Regret, And Redemption. One of the Various Major [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Gods\|Gods]].
	When Valimor was young he tried to raise up against his father, Aureon, however Valimor failed in this task. As punishment he was banished to the mortal realm, only to return as soon as he filled a Sack with special feathers.
		To gain a feather Valimor has to do a good deed, after the deed is done a beam of light will shine down with a singular feather.
