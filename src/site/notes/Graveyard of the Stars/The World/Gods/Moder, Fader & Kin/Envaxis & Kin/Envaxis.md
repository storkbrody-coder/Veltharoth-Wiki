"The Mirror Eyed Warden". Third eldest child of [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Moder, Fader & Kin/Moder\|Moder]] & [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Moder, Fader & Kin/Fader\|Fader]]. God of Knowledge, Secrets, Judgment & Envy.
Envaxis resides in a separate dimension than the rest of the gods, unable to leave.
In this dimension Envaxis has been able to gain unparalleled knowledge. One of the Various Major [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Gods\|Gods]].
