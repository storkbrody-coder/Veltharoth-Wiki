"The Queen of the Depths" or "The Bitch Queen". Umberlee is the daughter of Thalorion and goddess of Storms, The Deep, & Sea Monsters.  She is commonly worshipped and pay tribute by sailors, since if she isn't she shall destroy the sailor's ship & kill them. One of the Various Major [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Gods\|Gods]].
