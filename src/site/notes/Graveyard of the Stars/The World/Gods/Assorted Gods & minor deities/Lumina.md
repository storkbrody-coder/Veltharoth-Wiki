"The Glimmering Moon" Lumina was the goddess of the moon,  and night. She gave her life some 1000 years ago to seal away a dark god, preventing them from conquering [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Dimensions/The Mundane Plane/The Mundane Plane\|The Mundane Plane]]. One of the Various primordial [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Gods\|Gods]].
