Solaris is one of the primordial [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Gods\|Gods]] within Veltharoth. Most of the time Solaris is unacting only moving with the day cycle. Though at one point he descended from his overlook from the heavens to strike a spear of living light into the [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Veltharoth/Natural Features/Galewyr River\|Galewyr River]].
