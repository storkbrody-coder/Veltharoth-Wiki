"The Embermother" Goddess of the forge, blacksmithing & Machinery. Mother to [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Hearth & Kin/Kaedor\|Kaedor]].
The town of Hearthshade is named after her. One of the Various Major [[Graveyard of the Stars/The World/Gods/Gods\|Gods]].
